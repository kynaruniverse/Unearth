# Unearth User Guide

---

# 1. What Is Unearth?

Unearth is a pattern-driven chaos companion that helps you discover where you most frequently misplace items.

---

# 2. Getting Started

Step 1:
Open unearthapp.co.uk

Step 2:
Add your commonly lost item (e.g., Keys).

Step 3:
Tap “I’ve Lost It” when misplacing the item.

Step 4:
After finding it, log the location.

Over time, Unearth will rank likely hiding spots.

---

# 3. Install As App (PWA)

On iPhone:
- Open in Safari.
- Tap Share.
- Tap “Add to Home Screen.”

On Android:
- Open in Chrome.
- Tap menu (⋮).
- Tap “Install App.”

---

# 4. Stats Page

Shows:
- Total losses
- Most chaotic item
- Most frequent hiding spot
- Badges earned

# 6. FAQ

Q: Is my data stored online?
A: V1 stores data only on your device.

Q: What happens if I clear browser data?
A: Your logs will be erased (until cloud sync in V2).

Q: Does this replace hardware trackers?
A: No. It reveals behavior patterns.
